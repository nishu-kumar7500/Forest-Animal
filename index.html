भारत के कुछ विशेष और प्रसिद्ध मंदिर

Kedarnath Temple
  • Location: Uttarakhand, India, in the Himalayas
  • Built: Original structure attributed to Pandavas (c.3100 BCE), present temple built in the 8th century by Adi Shankaracharya
  • Who Built: Pandavas (legend), Adi Shankaracharya (current temple)
  • How It Is Created: The temple is made of massive stone slabs, assembled without mortar, exhibiting ancient Himalayan architecture.
  • Special Details: The temple survived many natural disasters and glaciation, signifying its remarkable engineering.

केदारनाथ मंदिर उत्तराखंड में हिमालय की खूबसूरत घाटियों में स्थित है। यह मंदिर आदि शंकराचार्य द्वारा आठवीं शताब्दी में पुनर्निर्मित किया गया था।

Mahakal Temple
  • Location: Ujjain, Madhya Pradesh, India
  • Built: Earliest references to 4th century BCE; present structure dates to 6th century AD
  • Who Built: Kumarasena and others
  • How It Is Created: Classic North Indian architecture with grand shikhar and intricate carvings.
  • Special Details: Known for its Bhasma aarti and Jyotirlinga significance.
Amarnath Guffa (Cave)
  • Location: Jammu & Kashmir, India
  • Built: Worshipped since antiquity; natural cave formation
  • Who Built: Attributed to Maharishi Bhrigu and rediscovery by Buta Malik (legend)
  • How It Is Created: Natural cave containing an ice stalagmite Shiva Lingam
  • Special Details: Ice lingam size varies; important site of pilgrimage.

अमरनाथ गुफा जम्मू-कश्मीर में स्थित एक प्राकृतिक गुफा है, जिसमें शिवलिंग हिमांशु से बना होता है।

Shree Ram Temple (Ayodhya)
  • Location: Ayodhya, Uttar Pradesh, India
  • Built: Originally 11th century, reconstructed multiple times, current temple completed in 2024.
  • Who Built: Various historical patrons; present temple by Shri Ram Janmabhoomi Teerth Kshetra Trust.
  • How It Is Created: Traditional North Indian architecture with pink sandstone and intricate carvings.
  • Special Details: Birthplace of Lord Ram, grand pilgrimage site with millions visitors yearly.
