Top 5 Animals

Royal Bengal Tiger

The national animal of India. Known for its power and grace. Critically endangered due to poaching and habitat loss.

Lion

The king of the jungle, lions are known for their majestic manes and social pride structures. Mostly found in Africa.

Elephant

The largest land mammal. Highly intelligent and emotional, elephants are known for their memory and social bonds.

Dog

Man’s best friend. Loyal, intelligent, and domesticated for thousands of years, dogs come in various breeds worldwide.

Buffalo

Strong and sturdy animals used in farming and transport. Vital to rural agriculture in many Asian and African regions.

Top 5 Forests

Amazon Rainforest

The largest rainforest on Earth, covering multiple South American countries. Home to vast biodiversity and the Amazon River.

Congo Basin

The second-largest rainforest in the world, located in central Africa. Rich in wildlife like gorillas and elephants.

Taiga (Boreal Forest)

Spans across Canada, Russia, and Scandinavia. Known for coniferous trees and long, cold winters. Largest land biome.

Sundarbans

The world’s largest mangrove forest. Found in India and Bangladesh, home to Royal Bengal Tigers and unique flora.

Daintree Rainforest

Located in Australia, it's one of the oldest rainforests on Earth. Known for unique flora and fauna including cassowaries.
