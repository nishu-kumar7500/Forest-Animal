Indian Temples Information

Kedarnath Temple
  • Location: Uttarakhand, India, in the Himalayas
  • Built: Original structure attributed to Pandavas (c.3100 BCE), present temple built in the 8th century by Adi Shankaracharya
  • Who Built: Pandavas (legend), Adi Shankaracharya (current temple)
  • How It Is Created: The temple is made of massive stone slabs, assembled without mortar, exhibiting ancient Himalayan architecture.
  • Special Details: The temple survived many natural disasters and glaciation, signifying its remarkable engineering.

केदारनाथ मंदिर उत्तराखंड में हिमालय की खूबसूरत घाटियों में स्थित है। यह मंदिर आदि शंकराचार्य द्वारा आठवीं शताब्दी में पुनर्निर्मित किया गया था।
